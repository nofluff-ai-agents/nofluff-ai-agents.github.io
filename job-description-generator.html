NovaTech MENA

Series B Technology Company · 400 employees · Dubai, UAE

About us

At NovaTech MENA, we're building the infrastructure that powers the next generation of digital commerce across the Middle East. We're a team of 400 curious, driven people who believe that the best work happens when people feel trusted, challenged, and genuinely supported. Our culture is built on transparency, speed, and a deep commitment to each other's growth.

The opportunity

We're looking for a Senior HR Business Partner to join our People team at a pivotal moment in our growth. You'll work closely with our Engineering and Product leadership to build the people frameworks that will take us from 400 to 1,000 employees over the next two years. This is a high-impact, high-visibility role for someone who thrives at the intersection of strategy and execution.

What you'll do
  • Partner with Engineering and Product VPs to design and deliver people strategies aligned to business goals
  • Lead talent planning cycles including workforce planning, succession, and organisational design
  • Coach and advise senior leaders on performance, team dynamics, and people challenges
  • Drive the end-to-end performance management process across your business units
  • Analyse people data to identify trends and make proactive recommendations to leadership
  • Partner with the Talent Acquisition team on senior hiring, onboarding, and retention initiatives
  • Champion an inclusive culture by embedding DEI practices into every people process
What you bring
  • 6+ years of HR Business Partner experience, ideally in a technology or high-growth environment
  • Proven ability to influence senior stakeholders and navigate complex organisational dynamics
  • Strong analytical skills — you use data to tell stories and drive decisions
  • Experience working in the UAE or broader GCC region is a strong advantage
  • Fluency in English; Arabic is a plus
HRBP Talent Planning Org Design People Analytics Arabic (nice to have) GCC Experience (nice to have)
What we offer
  • Competitive salary benchmarked to UAE market rates + equity participation
  • Flexible hybrid working — 3 days in our Dubai Marina office
  • Annual learning budget of AED 8,000 for your professional development
  • Comprehensive health insurance for you and your dependents
  • 30 days annual leave + UAE public holidays

We welcome applications from people of all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives. We believe diverse teams build better products and create more inclusive workplaces. If you're excited about this role but don't meet every requirement, we still encourage you to apply.
